About

I help couples and individuals work through life challenges. I use a caring approach while integrating effective treatment models to help people thrive in life. Areas of Focus Include: Couples Therapy for Desire Differences, Communication Issues, and Overcoming Traumatic events. I am open to working with individuals and couples who have traditional as well as non-traditional partnerships. I provide a supportive and safe space for individuals in the LGBTQIA+ community. I also specialize in helping young adults recover from childhood trauma and in empowering them to find their own path in life.
I utilize a strengths based approach to help you set clear and achievable goals. Clients can expect honest and supportive feedback to understanding and addressing issues in their life and relationships. I believe in providing a compassionate space to develop concrete tools and insights that will help you achieve your goals.
If you are feeling lost or stuck in life, feeling haunted by a past event, wish to feel closer to your partner or to improve your communication, please call me at (607) - 123 - 4567. We can set up a time to talk about what you are looking for and determine if I'm the right fit for you. Free 15 min phone consultations provided.
